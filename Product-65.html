Dental Etching Gel

Dental etching gel is a critical component in the preparation of tooth surfaces for bonding procedures. It ensures that the bonding agents adhere properly to the enamel or dentin, providing a strong and durable bond. This gel is easy to apply and rinse off, making it user-friendly for dental professionals. Its precise formulation guarantees effective etching without causing damage to the tooth structure.
